About Flux

At Flux Pilates, we believe in the power of conscious movement. Our philosophy centers on the transformative power of Pilates. We focus on enhancing alignment, cultivating flexibility, and strengthening the deep core – the powerhouse of your body. Our goal is to help you rediscover your physical balance, improve your posture, and move with greater ease and efficiency in your daily life.

Alignment

Discover precision and balance. We focus on proper form to enhance posture, reduce injury risk, and create graceful, efficient movement in your daily life.

Flexibility

Unlock your body's potential. Our classes lengthen and strengthen muscles simultaneously, improving your range of motion and releasing physical tension.

Deep Core

Build strength from within. We guide you to engage your deepest core muscles—the powerhouse of your body—for ultimate stability and power.

Meet Your Instructor

Monica

Owner & Certified Pilates Instructor

Monica, the owner of Flux, is a Barre and Pilates certified instructor who has been teaching movement for over 40 years. She is Stott Pulates trained in mat, reformer, barrels, stability chair, and special populations. SHe has successfully co-owned Osterville Pilates for 10 years in Cape Cod, MA.

Flux Pilates in Sherborn is her dream come true, a well equipped studio that provides a unique and individualized experience to each client, with the common goals of spine mobility, stability, and balance, improved range of motion, and deep core strength.
